-The project was a Healthcare decision making system win which interfaces(JAVA) for all the  users involved in a health-treatment procedure were involved (doctor,patient,Medical store).
- The patient could take appointments, view his reports.
- The doctor could manage his appointments and give reports and prescriptions.
He could also view a patients history.
- The medical store had access to only the prescription given by the doctor.
- All the data was maintained in a centralised database(MySQl).
